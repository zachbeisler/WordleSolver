------------------- WorldeSolver Help -------------------
1. Guesses:
	Enter the guess you input to Wordle
	Enter a guess of "help" to show this menu
	Enter a guess of "*" to display the current word list
	Enter a guess of "restart" to restart the round
2. Patterns:
	After inputting a guess to Wordle, input its response
	Gray Block <=> "x"
	Yellow Block <=> "?"
	Green Block <=> "o"
	I.E. Gray Gray Yellow Green Gray <=> "xx?ox"
